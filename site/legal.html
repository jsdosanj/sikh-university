Terms of Service

  • Educational purpose. Courses are provided for personal learning. Certificates of completion recognise progress on this Platform and are not accredited academic qualifications.
  • Accounts. If accounts are enabled, you are responsible for the activity under your account and for keeping access to your email secure (we use passwordless "magic-link" sign-in).
  • Acceptable use. Do not misuse the Platform, attempt to break its security, upload unlawful content, or harass others. Discussion and contributions must be respectful, especially given the sacred nature of much of the content.
  • Content & accuracy. We strive for accuracy and review course material, but provide content "as is" without warranty. Some courses are AI-drafted (see AI Disclosure) and reviewed by an editor. Report errors via Contact.

Privacy Policy

We practise data minimisation: we collect only what is needed to run the Platform, and we never sell personal data.

  • What we collect. If you create an account: your email address and your learning data (course progress, quiz results, certificates, and a display name you choose). If you contact us: the details you submit. Basic, privacy-respecting analytics may be collected in aggregate.
  • Why (lawful bases under GDPR). To provide the service you request (contract/consent), to keep it secure (legitimate interests), and to comply with law.
  • Cookies. We use only essential cookies/local storage needed for sign-in and to remember your progress. No advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.
  • Your rights (GDPR). Access, rectification, erasure ("right to be forgotten"), restriction, portability, and objection. You can export or delete your data from your account or by contacting us.
  • Your rights (CCPA/CPRA). Right to know, delete, correct, and opt out of "sale"/"sharing" — we do not sell or share personal information. We will not discriminate for exercising these rights.
  • Retention. We keep account data while your account is active and delete it on request or after prolonged inactivity.
  • Security. Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, least-privilege access, and the practices in our security model.
  • Children. Not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country); we do not knowingly collect their data.
  • International transfers & contact. Where data crosses borders we use appropriate safeguards. Privacy requests: Contact.

AI-content disclosure

Courses labelled Created by AI were drafted with the help of AI and reviewed by a Sikh University editor for accuracy. We label AI-assisted content for transparency. Sacred material is handled with care, but AI can err — please verify with authentic sources and report corrections.

Accessibility

We aim to meet WCAG 2.2 AA: semantic structure, keyboard navigation, sufficient contrast, and resilient, low-bandwidth pages. Accessibility is ongoing — if something is hard to use, tell us via Contact and we will fix it.
