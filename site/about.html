SikhUniversity

A space to dig deeper into Sikhi.

Sikh University is a free, open online university — built so that anyone, anywhere can study Sikhi seriously, from authentic sources, at their own pace.

Why we created Sikh University

Sikhi is deep. Its history spans five centuries, its scripture holds the voices of Gurus, Bhagats and Bhatts, and its philosophy speaks to the biggest questions of life. Yet for most people there has never been one welcoming, structured place to learn it — to move from a first question to real understanding.

We built Sikh University to be that place: a calm, organised home where a curious beginner and a serious student are equally welcome. No fees. No barriers. No sign-up needed to start learning. Just open, careful teaching — available to the whole world.

What the university offers

📚 Real courses, by topic

Dozens of courses across theology, philosophy, history, language, spirituality, music, the arts — and modern skills like AI.

🎓 Taught from the sources

Courses are drawn from the works of leading Sikh scholars and writers, each acting as the course's professor.

🧭 Guided learning paths

Ordered tracks take you from the basics to deeper study, so you always know what to learn next.

📝 Tests & certificates

Each course ends with a test. Pass with 80%+ to unlock the next course and earn a printable certificate.

♿ Open & accessible

Read-aloud, larger text, high-contrast mode, offline access — built so everyone can use it.

🌍 Free, forever for now

Every course is free today. We may add paid offerings later, but open access stays at our core.

How it works

  1. Browse or follow a path.
    Pick a topic, search the catalogue, or follow a guided learning path.
  2. Learn at your pace.
    Work through lessons written in clear English, with key terms in Punjabi (Gurmukhi).
  3. Take notes & track progress.
    Make notes as you go, build a streak, and earn badges on your dashboard.
  4. Pass the test, earn a certificate.
    Score 80%+ to complete a course and unlock what comes next.
