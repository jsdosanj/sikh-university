# Curriculum & course catalogue (initial)

Organized as **schools**, each with tracks; courses are self-paced with video, readings,
quizzes, discussion, and a certificate of completion. Scholars author/maintain courses in Studio.

## School of Sikhi
- **Foundations of Sikhi** — Who was Guru Nanak Sahib; the ten Gurus; Sri Guru Granth Sahib Ji
  as eternal Guru; core beliefs (Ik Onkar, Naam, Seva, equality).
- **Gurbani & Gurmat** — reading Gurbani, key Banis, Gurmat philosophy, Vaaran, contextual meaning.
- **Sikh History** — Guru period; the Khalsa; Misls; Sikh Empire; colonial era; 1947 & after; diaspora.
- **Gurmukhi & Punjabi** — script, reading, pronunciation, conversational Punjabi (beginner→advanced).
- **Kirtan & Raag** — shabad kirtan basics, raag, tabla/harmonium fundamentals (video-led).
- **Rehat & Practice** — Sikh way of life, Nitnem, the 5 Ks, sangat & pangat, life ceremonies.
- **Comparative & Contemporary** — Sikhi and ethics, science, social justice, interfaith.

## School of Modern Skills
- **AI Literacy for Everyone** — what AI is, how LLMs work, prompting, using AI responsibly & ethically.
- **AI Builder Track** — Python basics → working with APIs → building simple AI apps → agents.
- **Data & Digital Foundations** — spreadsheets→data thinking, the internet, online safety.
- **Cybersecurity Basics** — personal security, privacy, scams (aligns with our security mission).
- *(extensible: more 21st-century skills over time)*

## Imported / partnered (rights permitting)
- **OpenCourseWare imports** — selected CC-licensed MIT OCW / Harvard CS50 courses (CS, math,
  writing) as "Partner Courses," clearly attributed.
- **Basics of Sikhi** — embedded video series / co-branded track **pending partnership**.

## Pedagogy & access
- Multi-language from day one where possible (English + Punjabi; community translations later).
- Low-bandwidth mode (transcripts, downloadable readings) for global reach.
- Free certificates of completion; optional verified ID later.
- Accessibility (WCAG) and mobile-first.

## Authoring & quality
- **Scholar onboarding**: application → verification → Studio access → peer/board review → publish.
- **Review board** for doctrinal accuracy on Sikhi content; editorial standards for all courses.
- Versioned courses; "report a correction" on every page.
